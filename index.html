Who we are

ICB STRATEGIC PARTNERS registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission is a conglomerate of professional consultants regarded as one of the foremost strategy and transformational power houses delivering a tailored solution to private and Government Agencies in Nigeria. Our services focus on clients' most critical issues and opportunities: strategy, marketing, organization, operations, technology, transformation, digital, advanced analytics, corporate finance, mergers/acquisitions and sustainability across all industries and geographies.

We bring deep, functional expertise, but are known for our holistic perspective: we capture value across boundaries and between the silos of any organization. We have proven a multiplier effect from optimizing the sum of the parts, not just the individual pieces. Value creation inspires and propels us to action.
