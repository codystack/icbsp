Mr. Innocent Iyalla Harry

Mr. Innocent Iyalla Harry is a foremost financial expert and an evangelist of the social business model with which, through RIMA, he oversaw and funded within 5 years, the growth of over 10,000 small businesses in Rivers State. He holds a B.A (Hons) in Economics from the Lincoln University, and a Masters’ degree in International Banking and Financial Studies from Herriot-Watt University, Edinburgh both in the United Kingdom. His professional career traversed the Banking and financial institutions within and outside the Country for 30 years and these include; Chase Merchant Bank, AVC Funds Limited as Assistant Manager and Head of International Operations in 1987, Nigeria International Bank (Citibank) in 1989 as an Assistant Manager, Institutional Banking Group and later Manager, Head Office Branch Treasury Group, Nigerian- American Merchant Bank in 1994 as a Senior Manager and Head of the Port-Harcourt Branch, First City Monument Bank limited as Vice President/Head Port-Harcourt Branch in 1996-1998, and, Fortune International Bank PIc in 2001 as Assistant Vice President/ Group Head, Public Sector and Business Development South, Treasurer and later Corporate Strategist.

He functioned in several other capacities in the Bank before leaving in December 2005. He worked briefly as Group Executive Director, Zinox Technologies Limited Lagos, where he contributed to the strategic repositioning of the company across West Africa and thereafter, became the Managing Consultant, Dilann International Limited, moved on to become the Managing Director/Chief Executive of TMC Savings and Loans Limited (Mortgage Bankers) a member of the l.E.I Group. In July 2010, he was appointed the Managing Director/CEO of the Rivers State Microfinance Agency, RIMA; with a mandate to reposition and develop the microfinance sub- sector in Rivers State Nigeria; a mandate, which he passionately pursued and executed for 5 years.

Mr. Harry is among others, a professional member of both the British and Nigerian Institutes of Management, a member of the Institute of Directors of Nigeria and the UK where he also doubled as a member of the Governing Council of the Institute of Directors, Nigeria for 3years. Innocent has been a committed and passionate Rotarian for the past 32 years. He is the Charter President of the Rotary Club of Port Harcourt Cosmopolitan and a Two Star Paul Harris Fellow of the Rotary International, a Motivational Speaker, and a dedicated knight of the Catholic Church,

Iyalla Harry as he is fondly called is a corporate restructurer, a tested and proven asset and liability manager; dedicated team leader who seeks the success of his team members and enjoys more challenges in the finance and corporate world. He is a driver and apostle of sound corporate governance and accountability, and a keen observer of the International Money Market. He is an alumnus of the School of African Microfinance, Kenya, Johnson Institute of Responsible Governance, University of Pittsburg USA, and the Strategic Leadership for Microfinance of the Harvard Business School, USA.

Iyalla Harry is a tested and proven family man, a loving and doting father; he is married to his adorable wife Anne and together they are blessed with four (4) wonderful Children; a Medical Doctor, a Lawyer, a Drilling Engineer and another budding Lawyer. He currently serves on the Board of Dilann International Limited as Chairman, and was recently elected Vice President of The African SME Roundtable. Mr. Harry is the Founder and Principal Partner of ICB Strategic Partners; one of the foremost strategy and transformational power houses delivering custom made solutions.
