FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What approach do you adopt in rendering services?

    We have genuine commitment to the highest quality standards of professional service grounded in long term, partner-led client relationships. We strive to provide imaginative yet pragmatic advice, and solution derived from a thorough understanding of our clients' needs. These commitments are underpinned by our crafted methods of delivering quality services by our professional staff and our depth of skill and expertise in consulting and audit practice.

  • How do you manage your clients?

    Client satisfaction and consistent high-quality work are at the heart of our business strategy. This is reflected in the quality of work executed and the mutually beneficial relationships we enjoy with our clients. We work with each client on the understanding that it is unique, with its own set of needs, priorities, and expectations. We are fortunate to have a diverse client base, such that there is no typical client. This provides a rich pool of experience that we use to full advantage in solving our clients' problems. We invest heavily in training; IT and communications, which enable us to provide our clients with nothing but the best.

  • How do you estimate and quote for your services?

    Our fees are computed on the basis of time spent on your affairs by the principals and our staff, and on the levels of skill and responsibility involved. Where there is no standard contract rate, we negotiate with our clients based on the industry benchmark and estimate the professional service fee required. We request a deposit of 50% of the agreed fees at the date of commencement of the engagement, and the balance at completion.

  • What processes are involved in your service delivery?

    Once a client indicates interest in any of our services, we recommend they write us an offer and appointment letter. We respond with a letter of acceptance, stating our readiness to render the service indicated. Based on the specific service required, we send a letter of engagement to the client stating the terms and the agreed fee for the service. Prior to commencing the engagement, we send a notification letter intimating the client the date of commencement.

    We hold meetings on commencement and exit with the client to discuss approach and other related issues. We conduct examination, analysis, and deploy other related test methodology to achieve the intended objectives. We draft engagement report and present same to management at the end of the exercise.
