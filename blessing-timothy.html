Barr. Blessing Timothy

Barrister Blessing Timothy is a legal practioner and member of the Nigeria Bar Association, African Women Lawyers Association and International Federation of Women Lawyers. She is a young, dynamic and consummate lawyer, an advocate of social justice. She is PHD Law student with Rivers State University She is Lecturer 11, Rivers State University, Nkpolu, Port Harcourt, 2009 Till Date and An Associate Partner, Esquires of Owubokiri & Co. Port Harcourt, 2011 Till date.

In a past engagement with the Bayelsa State Development Agency, Barrister Blessing was responsible for resource planning and resolution of issues with the client. She identified the existing policies, the agreement terms and pertinent laws and regulations to determine the nature, timing and extent of procedures to test for errors, fraud, and illegal acts that provide reasonable assurance of detecting both intentional and unintentional instances of noncompliance with policies, agreement terms and applicable laws and regulations that could have a material effect on operation of business entities.

She reviewed compliance with policy, applicable laws and regulations of agencies of the Federal and State Governments. Contract agreements with National and International Agencies as well as strategic partners. In other engagements, she played an active role in the mediation between the SPDC and representatives of Rivers State communities such as in the implementation of the GMoU in 2007. In other previous engagement, she successfully deployed a modest Alternative Dispute Resolution in formal cases filed in the court and anchored the mediation of disparate parties of PDP in a case filed in court.
